Paneer Ki Sabji

Description

Paneer is an Indian cheese made from curdled milk. It's fresh, doesn't melt, and has a mild flavor. You'll find it in many curries and veggie dishes.

Paneer is like a firm, spongy tofu made from curdled milk. It absorbs flavors well and holds its shape in curries, stir fries, or even fried snacks.

Ingredients:

  • Ghee or vegetable oil - 3 tablespoons
  • Cashews - 1/2 cup
  • Almonds - 1/4 cup
  • Green cardamoms - 3
  • Onions - 2 medium, chopped
  • Green chilies - 1 (optional, for spice)
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Tomatoes - 4 medium, chopped
  • Red chili powder - 1 1/2 teaspoons
  • Coriander powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cloves - 2
  • Cinnamon stick - 1 inch
