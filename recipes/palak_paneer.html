Palak Paneer

Description

Palak paneer is a vibrant green curry made with spinach and paneer cheese. It's a healthy and flavorful vegetarian dish popular in Indian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Ghee or vegetable oil - 3 tablespoons
  • Onions - 2 medium, chopped
  • Green chilies - 1 (optional, for spice)
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, chopped
  • Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cloves - 2
  • Cinnamon stick - 1 inch
  • Spinach (palak) - 2 cups, chopped
  • Paneer - 200 grams, cubed
  • Garam masala - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cream (optional) - 1 tablespoon
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
Steps
  1. Saut√© onions, spices, and ginger-garlic in ghee.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until softened and mushy.
  3. Blend the tomato mixture into a smooth puree.
  4. (Optional) Fry cashews and almonds (1/4 cup each) for a richer gravy (use leftover from Paneer Ki Sabzi if made first).
  5. Add the tomato puree, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the masala releases oil.
  6. Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted.
  7. Add water or vegetable stock to adjust the gravy consistency. Season with garam masala.
  8. Gently fold in the cubed paneer and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  9. (Optional) Add a splash of heavy cream for extra richness.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice, naan, or roti.
